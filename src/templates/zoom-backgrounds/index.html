ASPMG Zoom Backgrounds

Four on-brand virtual backgrounds at full HD (1920×1080) — three stills plus one animated video with a subtle particle network. Click Download on any design, then upload as a Zoom virtual background.

How to use in Zoom:
  1. Click Download PNG (still) or Download Video (animated) on the design you want.
  2. In Zoom, open Settings → Backgrounds & Effects.
  3. Click the + next to Virtual Backgrounds → Add Image or Video, then select the downloaded file. Zoom auto-loops video backgrounds.
  4. If the logo / text looks reversed in your self-view: open Settings → Video and uncheck “Mirror my video.” Zoom only mirrors your local preview — other participants always see the background correctly.
