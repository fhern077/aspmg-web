ASPMG Zoom Backgrounds

Three on-brand virtual backgrounds at full HD (1920×1080). Click Download PNG on any design to save it, then upload as a Zoom virtual background.

How to use in Zoom:
  1. Click Download PNG on the design you want.
  2. In Zoom, open Settings → Backgrounds & Effects.
  3. Click the + next to Virtual Backgrounds and select the downloaded PNG.
  4. If the logo / text looks reversed in your self-view: open Settings → Video and uncheck “Mirror my video.” Zoom only mirrors your local preview — other participants always see the background correctly.
