4
Usage Guidelines

Keep the brand consistent across all communications.

Do
  • Use the logo on white or navy backgrounds
  • Maintain clear space around the logo
  • Use brand colors for all documents
  • Use DM Serif Display for headings
  • Use Sora for body text and UI
  • Include contact info on all materials
Don't
  • Stretch, rotate, or distort the logo
  • Place the logo on busy photo backgrounds
  • Change the logo colors
  • Use fonts other than DM Serif / Sora
  • Add drop shadows or effects to the logo
  • Use low-resolution logo files for print
